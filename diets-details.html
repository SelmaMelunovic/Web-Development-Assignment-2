Diet Details

Fruit Salad

A healthy, refreshing mix of seasonal fruits.

Ingredients:
  • Apple, Banana, Orange, Grapes
  • Honey, Cinnamon (optional)
Instructions:

Chop fruits, mix, and add honey and cinnamon if desired.

Keto Meal: Grilled Chicken with Broccoli

High-protein, low-carb meal for keto followers.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken Breast, Broccoli
  • Olive Oil, Garlic Powder, Paprika
Instructions:

Grill chicken, steam broccoli, and serve together.

Mediterranean Salad

Fresh and vibrant salad with veggies and feta cheese.

Ingredients:
  • Cucumber, Tomato, Red Onion, Olives
  • Feta Cheese, Olive Oil, Red Wine Vinegar
Instructions:

Mix ingredients and drizzle with olive oil and vinegar.

Vegan Buddha Bowl

A nourishing bowl with quinoa, roasted sweet potato, and chickpeas.

Ingredients:
  • Quinoa, Chickpeas, Sweet Potato
  • Avocado, Spinach, Tahini
Instructions:

Assemble ingredients and drizzle with tahini dressing.

Vegan Lentil Soup

A hearty and vegan-friendly soup with lentils and veggies.

Ingredients:
  • Lentils, Carrots, Celery, Onion, Garlic
  • Vegetable Broth, Cumin, Turmeric
Instructions:

Cook lentils and veggies in broth with spices until tender.

Paleo Beef Stir-fry

A quick stir-fry with beef, vegetables, and coconut oil.

Ingredients:
  • Lean Beef, Bell Peppers, Broccoli
  • Coconut Oil, Coconut Aminos, Garlic
Instructions:

Stir-fry beef and vegetables, season with spices and coconut aminos.
